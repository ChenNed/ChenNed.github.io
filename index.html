Xueqin Chen is a final-year Ph.D. candidate at School of Information and Software Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (since 2017 fall), under the supervision of Prof. Fengli Zhang and Dr. Fan Zhou. He also joined LIACS, Leiden University as a Ph.D candidate in 2019 fall, and supervised by Prof. Marcello Bonsangue. His research broadly spans applied deep learning and social computing, with a focus on information diffusion modeling, graph representation learning, and fake news detection. Currently, he is working on developing deep-learning based model to extract the multi-scale information from information cascades. Besides that, he is also working on achieving efficient and interpretable fake news detection by using the propagation patterns. If you are interested in modeling information cascades by using deep learning technicals, highly recommended: Awesome-DL-Information-Cascades-Modleing.
