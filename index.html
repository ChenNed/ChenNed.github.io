I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Delft University of Technology, working with the team of AidroLab at the Department of Civil Engineering and Geosciences and Electrical Engineering, Mathematics and Computer Science, hosted by Dr. Riccardo Taormina. Before that, I was a Ph.D. candidate at the School of Information and Software Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (since 2017 fall), under the supervision of Prof. Fengli Zhang and Prof. Fan Zhou, and also joined LIACS, Leiden University as a Ph.D candidate in 2019 fall, and supervised by Prof. Marcello Bonsangue. My research interests lie primarily in the application of graph representation learning. Recently, I focused on deploying GNN in the water network. If you are interested in modeling information cascades by using deep learning technicals, highly recommended: Awesome-DL-Information-Cascades-Modleing.
